Key Information

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) game developed by Moonton and released on July 14, 2016. It is available on iOS and Android and runs on the Unity engine. The game features 5v5 battles, where players choose heroes with different skills and work as a team to defeat their opponents. It combines strategy and action, making it exciting and competitive. Mobile Legends is also a big name in esports, with tournaments like the Mobile Legends Professional League (MPL). Its popularity even led to a nomination for The Game Award for Best Esports Game.

  • Initial Release Date:July 14, 2016
  • Developer: Moonton
  • Publisher: Moonton
  • Engine: Unity
  • Platforms: iOS, Android
  • Genres: MOBA, Real-time Strategy, ARPG
  • Nominations: The Game Award for Best ESports Game

Characters

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang features a wide variety of heroes that players can choose from, each with unique abilities and roles. The heroes are divided into different classes, allowing players to create balanced teams for their 5v5 battles. Here are the main types of heroes in the game:

  • Tank: High durability, absorbs damage
  • Fighter: Balanced attack and defense
  • Assassin: High burst damage
  • Mage: Uses magic attacks
  • Marksman: Long-range attacks
  • Support: Healer

Guides

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is divided into three lanes, Top, Middle, and Bottom. Each lane has minions that push toward the enemy’s base, and players need to farm them for gold and experience. The main objective is to destroy the enemy’s towers and base while protecting your own. Who every reaches or destroys the main tower wins the game. As a beginner, it’s important to understand your hero’s role, whether it’s a tank, marksman, mage, support, or assassin, and focus on objectives rather than just chasing kills.
