Pad Thai Recipe

Pad Thai is a delicious Thai dish known for its rich flavors with its sweet, sour, salty, and spicy taste.

Ingredient:

  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 100g shrimp or tofu
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 2 lime

Method:

  1. Boil the rice noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside
  2. In a small bowl, mix fish sauce, tamarind paste, sugar, lime juice, and chili powder to prepare the sauce.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a pan over medium heat and cook the shrimp (or tofu) for 2-3 minutes, then remove and set aside.
  4. Add another tbsp of oil to the same pan. Saut√© the minced garlic for 30 seconds, then add the beaten egg and scramble it.
  5. Return the cooked noodles to the pan, pour the prepared sauce over them, and toss well to coat evenly.
  6. Add the cooked shrimp (or tofu), bean sprouts, and green onions to the pan. Stir everything together for a minute.
  7. Serve the Pad Thai hot, garnished with crushed peanuts and lime wedges on the side.
